Art Gallery Inventory Management System

Help & Guide

List Page

View all inventory items in a list format. Use the search bar to find specific items by name. Pull down to refresh the list.

Add Page

Add new items to your inventory. Fill in all required fields (marked with *) and click "Add Item" to save.

  • Item Name: Must be unique
  • Category: Electronics, Furniture, Clothing, Tools, Miscellaneous
  • Stock Status: In stock, Low stock, Out of stock

Manage Page

Update or delete existing items. Search for an item first, then modify its details or delete it.

  • Update: Change any field and save
  • Delete: Requires confirmation

Featured Items

Mark items as "Featured" to highlight them. Featured items appear on the Add page for quick access.

About

This app connects to a remote API at Southern Cross University. Your data is stored securely on SCU servers.
